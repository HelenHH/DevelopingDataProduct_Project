About

Snowing in Chicago winter is a serious weather event for the local residents and the authorities. The winter months linger from Nove to April,

This simple, interactive app provides an unique opportunities for the viewers to search for historical snow fall in Chicago winter from 1958 winter till Nov, 2014.

Monthly total snow falls (inches) and concurrent monthly average air temperture (as max temp, min temp and average temp) are analyzed .

Searchable information are displayed as 'Snow Fall' plot, 'Air Temp' plot and also summaried in table output format under 'Data' tab.

(Data used for the project are obtained from National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NORR, http://www.noaa.gov)
